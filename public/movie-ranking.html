My top 3 movies of all time

Hum Saath Saath Hain

This is a family oriented movie, shows how much family is important and they have beautifully potraied siblings relation ship.

This is a real story of an indian soldier who lost his life in the kargil war, with his efforts and sacrifice he saved country from a big threat.

Cinema Bandi

This is a story of an auto driver and the camera, a passenger forgets there camera in the auto of that auto driver and he founds the camera and decided to shoot a movie with that, story revolves around shooting very funny and crazy.
